<section class="text-block">
  We spent first 3 days on the main island, Viti Levu, in a resort called Yatule.
  It has a nice long white beach, but unfortunately the sea was rough and there were big waves the whole time we stayed there.
</section>

<section class="text-block">
  After Yatule we went to our main destination - Blue Lagoon Beach Resort. It's located on the Nacula island in the chain of Yasawa islands to the north of main island. The transfer boat takes about 4.5 hours and it stops at many other resorts on the way.
</section>
<section class="text-block">
  The weather at Blue Lagoon was better and there were no waves, probably because it's a lagoon :)
</section>
